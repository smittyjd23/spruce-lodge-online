Restaurants & Groceries

Where to eat and shop nearby

Restaurants in the Valley

  • Canaan BBQ

    Open seasonally, so check hours before going. Delicious barbeque in the Valley.

  • Mamma Mia Pie and Pasta

    Pizza and Italian dishes in the valley between Timberline and Canaan Valley Resort State Park.

Restaurants in Davis

  • Sirianni's Cafe

    A landmark Italian restaurant in Davis, established in 1988 within a historic century-old steamship office. Known for its old-school atmosphere and quirky decor.

  • Moguls of Davis

    A sit down restaurant with dishes including steak, seafood, and pasta.

  • Hellbender Burritos

    The best burritos in the Appalachian Mountains. You won't regret going.

  • Milo's Restaurant and Bar

    Open for dinner every day and lunch and dinner on select days, Milo's serves a variety of dishes from burgers to poke bowls.

  • Wicked Wilderness Pub and Parlor

    Another local restaurant with reliable burgers, sandwiches, and assorted fare. Also a great selection of local and non-local beers.

Restaurants in Thomas

  • Purple Fiddle

    A legendary music venue and deli is the heart of Thomas, hosting live bluegrass and mountain music almost every night of the year. Beyond the music, it's a family-friendly gathering spot where you can grab a sandwich, locally made ice cream, or a craft beer.

  • Tip Top

    A cornerstone of Thomas's modern revival, this stylish coffee shop serves expertly crafted espresso drinks and nitro cold brew alongside a selection of spirits and small-batch chocolate. It's the perfect place to start your morning with a bagel sandwich or unwind in the afternoon surrounded by artsy, local vibes.

Brew Pubs

  • Stumptown Ales

    Local beers, brewed in Davis.

  • Big Timber Logging Camp Taproom

    An Elkins, WV brewery with an outpost in Davis. The Logging Camp Taproom overlooks the Blackwater River in Davis, WV.

  • Mountain State Brewing Company

    Situated between Davis and Thomas, Mountain State has a selection of beers, pizzas, and sandwiches.

Groceries

  • Shop N Save

    The only full-service grocery store in the area.

  • Mountain Owl

    Open seasonally, so check hours before going. Offers a selection of sandwiches, pastries, coffee, wine, and craft beers.

  • BFS

    A gas station convenience store with a Subway inside. You can find a full array of snacks and beverages here.
